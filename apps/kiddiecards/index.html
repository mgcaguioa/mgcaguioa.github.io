Description

Little Learners Cards is a colorful, kid-friendly flashcard app designed to make early learning fun and interactive! Perfect for toddlers and preschoolers, this app helps children recognize letters, numbers, animals, and more through vibrant images and audio.

✅ Adorable flashcards for ABCs, numbers, animals, colors, and more!

✅ Child-safe interface with big buttons and no confusing menus

✅ Fun sounds and clear voice-overs to boost learning

✅ Offline mode – play anytime, anywhere

✅ Non-personalized, kid-friendly ads (no tracking!)



👪 Designed for families and young children with love and care.
