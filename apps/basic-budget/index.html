Description

Take control of your finances with Basic Budget – the simple, flexible, and powerful way to manage your money!

⭐ Simple and easy to use: No more confusing interfaces – just straightforward budgeting

⭐ Customizable budgets: Effortlessly create and manage lists of your expenses and incomes

⭐ Quick summaries: Instantly view totals to see where your money is going

⭐ Drag & reorder: Need to prioritize an item? Simply drag and reorder your lists

⭐ Mark items as paid or received: Keep track of what’s settled with a single tap

⭐ Star favorites: Automatically add starred items to future budgets for convenience

⭐ Visualize with charts: Our clean, simple charts give you a clear view of your financial health

Whether you're planning your daily expenses or long-term savings, Basic Budget has you covered. Start managing your money the easy way today!
